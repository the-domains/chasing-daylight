Chasing Daylight / Cycling in North Negros, Philippines

Seven Falls at Gawahon

Accessible right from the end of the concrete road, this is the first falls of seven in the park. Cyclists who succeed in slaying this difficult climb will have a refreshing reward. Hike up the trail for about half an hour more and you will find the Fifth and Sixth Falls consisting of the highest drops in the series and where the water breaks into twin falls.
