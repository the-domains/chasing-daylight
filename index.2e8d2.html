Helios Solar Farm, Cadiz City

The largest solar farm in Southeast Asia, and currently the 7th largest in the world, Helios provides 132 Megawatts of solar power to the grid. Enough to power 70,000 households.

Mount Kanlandog, Murcia

Riding up the foothills of Mt. Kanlandog to the east of Bacolod allows riders wonderful views of the mountain ranges surrounding Murcia town to the west, and the majestic Mount Kanlaon to the east.
